Hi! My name is Sana and welcome to this colorful space I call my portfolio. Here you can find my artwork, music, and of course, my first website made from scratch! Below is what I am currently working on and if you would like to see more of my visual art, navigate to my art page at the top!

I am a freshman in my first semester at Cal and I created this website as a final project for the Web Design Decal class I took! I am an intended art practice major and am currently pursuing the design innovation certificate. I have always wanted to mix my love for computer science and art and classes at Cal in web design and animation allow me to do that in a very creative, clean manner.

Right now my graphic design skills are being applied to Fashion and Student Trends UC Berkeley, where I create promotional material and online content for Instagram, Facebook, and their TikTok (@fastcal). I also create poster and merchandise designs for ASUC Superb at Berkeley, a club devoted to providing entertainment for the students in the community.

My most recent project is the development of an .io game with eight of my peers. The game, Blazer, was inspired by the game, Among Us which gathered popularity during the pandemic. Blazer is also an online multiplayer game that aims to helps kids bond and build relationships virtually. I am the sprite designer and am working on CGI graphics on blender.
